The Problem

Lecture comprehension breaks when visuals are missing

🎓
10+ million African university students

sit in lectures where visual aids are scarce, boards get erased, and slides fly by.

📱
Attention gets split instantly

Students Google concepts on their phones mid-lecture — and miss the next 3 concepts.

⚠️
Current tools stop at transcription

Existing tools (Otter.ai, NotebookLM) transcribe lectures. None of them help you understand while the lecturer is still explaining.

How It Works

Three steps from lecture audio to understanding

Step 1 — Prep

Before Class

Tell LectureLens what today's lecture is about, or upload your slides. Claude AI builds a knowledge base with concepts, diagrams, and definitions — ready in minutes.

Step 2 — Listen

During Class

Tap Start. LectureLens listens through your mic, transcribes in real time, and detects key concepts every few seconds using Claude Haiku.

Step 3 — Understand

In Real Time

Claude Sonnet generates custom visuals — mind maps, plots, formulas, comparison tables — appearing as glanceable cards. After class, get complete structured notes.

Core Capabilities

Designed for live comprehension, not passive transcripts

🧠

Real-Time Concept Detection

Claude Haiku scans your lecture transcript every few seconds and identifies the key concept being discussed.

📊

Dynamic Visual Generation

AI generates Mermaid mind maps, matplotlib plots, LaTeX formulas, and comparison tables custom to what your lecturer is saying.

🕸️

Live Concept Map

Watch a knowledge graph build itself as the lecture progresses — see how topics connect in real time.

📦

Smart Lecture Packs

Describe your topic or upload slides. Claude builds a course-specific knowledge base before class starts.

⚡

Glanceable Cards

Designed to be understood in a 2-second glance. Big diagrams, minimal text. Look down, understand, look back up.

📝

Auto-Generated Notes

Every concept, diagram, formula, and explanation is compiled into structured study notes after class.
